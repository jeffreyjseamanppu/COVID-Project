PPU Covid Checker


We created the Point Park Covid Checker during our Senior Project course in 2021. The goal here was to develop a covid cheacker, that the university currently lacked
in order to obtain covid data on the university community, along with provide insights for university admin to make bette, data-driven, mitigration policies.
This was created to combat the covid-19 virus, although can be applied to relatively any setting (K-12 Schools, University, and even Companies),
along with tailored to any illness or disease that is around such as flu, whooping cough, lice, and more.
